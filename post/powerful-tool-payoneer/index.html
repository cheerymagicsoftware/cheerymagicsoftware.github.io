PAYONEER: ALL-IN-ONE PLATFORM FOR GLOBAL GROWTH

Mon, Jun 10, 2019

Get paid for work done is naturally. But if your clients from different places in the world. You need an international powerful tool to get and receive money. Our company decided to use Payoneer. Why this system is popular and very powerful? Let’s show in examples.

When we finish the project and close contract in Upwork platform, then we get payments directly to prepaid debit card Payoneer. It easy setting in the account page. Go to Account->Settings->Get Paid. Click Add Method button and you’ll see next modal window

Choose Payoneer and you’ll redirect to Payoneer Area. Login or Register and then back to Upwork with the related payment method

After that, we can get money from our Payoneer Card in the local ATM.

Another interesting tool Global Payment Service. This service you can use as like your own bank account in a local bank for several currencies. Example, let’s start selling on eBay.

After registering, we must create a seller account. Then you must fill account seller form and get call or text message to your phone to verify. When you start selling on eBay you have limited 10 items and 500$ per month. Advice: you must upgrade the rating for good sells. Rating on eBay count for sell and buy items. So you can buy several items and grow up your rating.

To receive money from eBay your Payoneer account easy with Global Payment Service. Go to Home > My eBay > My Account > Personal Information > Financial Information and add “automatic payment method”.

To fill this fields go to payoneer accont Account > Global Payment Service

Choose the account and fill now eBay form.

During few days eBay get and recive back 0.01$ to verify your data.

Conclusion: Payoneer is a powerful platform to manage your finance over all of the worlds. Join!
